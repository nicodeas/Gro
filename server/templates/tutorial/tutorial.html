Tutorial

A quick guide to getting started

Upon starting the game you will be presented with your Gro-buddy, as well as three buttons to interact with.

They each provide a separate positive mental health activity for you to complete in order to help you build habits while helping your Gro-buddy grow.

The first activity is a mood journal entry, represented by the fertilizer icon.

For this activity, you are asked to provide a rating for your mood, which is then tracked in the app.

With each activity completed, your plant becomes happier!

The second activity is performing a guided breathing exercise, represented by a watering can.

For this activity, you breathe with a visual aid that assists you in taking slow, deep breaths in order to relieve stress.

The third activity is a body scan meditation, represented by a sun.

For this activity, you are given instructions to perform a body scan meditation to practice mindfulness.
